Features
---
Todo List
Have a general to-do list with a large overall task and subtasks, that can be checked off
    Checking off the large overall task will automatically check off subtasks
    Tasks can have due dates
    Tasks can be recurring 
Add a system where a large task can be broken down into subtasks

Next 60 Tracker
60 rounded boxes representing days. Every day will have a set amount of tasks.
Completing no tasks during a day makes that day red, some tasks yellow, most tasks light green, all deep green
Tasks can be manually assigned to certain days, or will use AI to distribute tasks based on their difficulty, due dates, and recurring tasks.

Pomodoro
Built in pomodoro timer, that will assign tasks/tasks can be selected to be worked on from the Next 60 Tracker. 
    History of how long the work/study session was is saved, what was completed is also saved, 
    shows the user their current study streak (how many days in a row used pomodoro),
    average study time
    longest streak
    total tasks completed
